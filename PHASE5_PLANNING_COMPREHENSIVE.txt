Payment System Types:
1. DIRECT PAYMENT
   - Customer pays to your bank account directly

2. JV PAYMENT (Joint Venture)
   - Customer pays to vendor's bank account (provided by you)
   - Vendor holds money temporarily
   - After verification → Settlement happens
   - Intense monitoring required

3. CREDIT
   - Customer buys on credit
   - Pays later from their account balance
   - Credit limit per customer
   - Partial payments allowed

4. CASH
   - Customer pays cash directly

================================================================================
                    DATA LIFECYCLE STRATEGY
================================================================================

HOT DATA (Current Year)
- Location: Main database
- Full indexes
- Access: Fast
- Storage: SSD/Primary DB

WARM DATA (1-2 years old)
- Location: Separate schema
- Read-only access
- Summarized data available
- Storage: Secondary DB

COLD DATA (2-7 years old)
- Location: Cloud archive (compressed)
- Accessed rarely
- For legal/audit purposes only
- Storage: S3/Azure Blob (geographically separated)

PURGE (7+ years old)
- Delete after retention period
- Keep legal audit trail
- Archive immutable log
